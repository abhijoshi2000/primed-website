Built by an athlete, for athletes

I'm Abhi Joshi — software engineer, Ironman 140.6 finisher, and marathon runner. I built Primed because I was tired of guessing whether to push hard or pull back on any given day.

After finishing an Ironman in 2025 and a marathon in 2022, I learned the hard way that the difference between a great training block and an injury often comes down to listening to your body. The problem is, most of us are bad at that.

So I built an app that does it for you. Primed takes the data your wearable already collects — HRV, sleep, recovery — and turns it into a clear readiness score and a workout plan that adapts to how you actually feel. No more spreadsheets, no more guessing.
